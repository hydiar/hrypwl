A website for me

Once simple, elegant and promising, the internet has now become a noisy place. Full of seemingly impossible to avoid marketing, intrusive tracking, and feeds competing for attention, it is now time to create my own quiet corner. No cookies. No ads. No AI. No flashy graphics or endless pop-ups. Just simple pages, built in the spirit of what the web had the potential to be. Personal and benign.

This site is a place to slow down. A place where content exists because I chose to put it here — not because it was optimised, monetised, or engineered for clicks. Every page is created by hand, and everything you see is here on purpose. I’m using this space to share the achievements I’m proud of, the things that spark my curiosity, and the ideas I want to explore. Projects, experiments, notes, and thoughts — some polished, some unfinished. I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as I enjoyed creating it.
